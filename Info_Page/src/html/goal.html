Our Goal

The Curriculum Planning Framework (CPF) is a tool that was first developed in 2008 and displayed the provincial outcomes in a poster format for educators. This organizational tool, when used in conjunction with curriculum guides and authorized resources, provides educators assistance with long-term planning. It's puspose was to:

- Assist with time managment and pacing of instruction
- Ensure complete delivery of the curriculum outcomes
- Assis with cross curricular planning.
